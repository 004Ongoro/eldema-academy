    About Eldema letap Academy

    Eldema Letap Academy is a community based educational institution started in the year 2005, it supports children of ages between 3 to 13 years mostly from humble backgrounds to achieve universal basic education at an affordable rate, for the past twenty years of its operation more than 10,000 children have benefited from the program and many more are looking for the opportunity but resources constrain them. Eldema letap Academy mostly depends on well-wishersand donors to run its daily activities. The school operates in Dagoretti Sub County, Nairobi County, Kenya and mostly serves the children from the areas of Mutu-ini, Waithaka, Kawangware, Riruta and Ngando. Currently the school has over 350 pupils.
